F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of units do you offer?

We offer four unit categories: Essence (Basic), Core Plus (Standard), Executive (Superior), and Prestige (Premium), each designed to suit different lifestyle needs and budgets.

Are your homes customizable?

Yes! Our Executive and Prestige units offer customization options, allowing you to personalize interiors, layouts, and smart home features.

What amenities are included in each unit?

Each unit comes with unique privileges. Essence includes essential features, Core Plus adds upgraded interiors, Executive includes smart home integration and premium finishes, while Prestige offers luxury living with private amenities.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with leading financial institutions to provide flexible financing plans tailored to your needs.

What security features do your homes have?

All our units include advanced security systems, with Prestige and Executive units offering smart surveillance, biometric access, and 24/7 monitoring.

Do you provide property management services?

Yes, we offer property management for maintenance, security, and rental assistance to ensure a hassle-free experience.
